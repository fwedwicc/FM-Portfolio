# FM Portfolio

FM Portfolio is an extensive showcase that delves into my personal background, interests, education, and achievements. It highlights my innovative and creative works, as well as my professional journey in the tech industry. This project serves as a comprehensive representation of my skills, experiences, and dedication to front-end development and UI/UX design.

## Sections

- **Home Page:** The landing page provides a warm introduction and overview of my portfolio.

  - **Hero:** A dynamic and visually appealing section that highlights key aspects of my work and invites visitors to explore further.
  - **About:** A brief overview of my background, interests, and journey in the tech industry.
  - **Education:** Details of my academic background and relevant coursework.
  - **Expertise:** Showcases my skills, GitHub statistics, and various certifications.
  - **Projects:** Features a visual highlight project with a button to view all my projects.
  - **Contact:** Provides information on how to get in touch with me.

- **All Projects:** This section includes a comprehensive list of all my notable projects, showcasing the breadth and depth of my work.

- **Project Overview:** Detailed case studies of individual projects, including low-fi and hi-fi, and also the outcome.
